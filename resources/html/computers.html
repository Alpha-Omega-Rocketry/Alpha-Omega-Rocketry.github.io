ZETA Flight Computer: Zeta in the Greek alphabet means spark of life. In our rockets it is truly the life of the system as a whole. There are multiple planned versions of ZETA. 1st generation (ZG1): This is a data collector for regular flights. Update: Due to Covid, I made a video on ZG1 and it was my 2020 Electronics project for 4H. In a turn of events it was given Champion at the county leval and selected for state! It went on to place top 3 at State. 2nd Generation (ZG2): This version was a test article for servo sweeps. ​ 3rd Generation (ZG3) This is our first fully operation TVC flight Computer. Complete with two pyro channels, servo outputs, BNO055, Teensy 4.1, and BME280. It is wirelessly controlled using nrf24l01 2.4 ghz. radios. ​
