This is my story

This is Jacob: Founder and President

I have always been intrigued by space and the vastness of it. I had always enjoyed reading about the moon landings and shuttle launches. As a Christian it boggled my mind that anyone could think it came about randomly. I saw space as something which attested to God's power! As a five year old, watching the planetarium show at the Creation Museum in Kentucky, I was enamored with space. As soon as I was old enough I became part of my local 4-H Aerospace club. Over many years I have made many model rockets. My goal would be to make high placing models. This would soon change as I became a teen. I got into the logistics of it. The methods and science behind the rockets. When I was 14 I met a friend who introduced me to SpaceX and high tech model rocketry. Particularly the guys at BPS.space. They all ignited my passion and dream of being a rocket scientist. Ultimately my dream is to go to college and Major in Aerospace Engineering and work at a respected aerospace organization. While continuing my rocket research. Starting with AOR, I decided to learn rocketry concepts in High School. The first and current project is Thrust Vectoring. You can help me achieve my dream!
